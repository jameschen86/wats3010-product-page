Learn about Discovering Eternity

The world has always been composed of four region. The supreme militant-driven nation to the west, the traditional magical academy to the east, the crime driven slum of the south, and technological advance metropolis of the north. In attempt to promote unification of the four nations, young Kari was selected to join a squad of explorers with three other young adults from the remaining three regions. The group makes a discovery of a lifetime, and ancient civilization predating all four nations. As her group returns to report their discover she finds one of their fellow members dead, with her and two other explorers as the prime suspect.

Chapter 1

They were a bad match. Kari had knew that when she first the other members of her group from other regions for a long expedition of exploring the unknown jungle that flourished in the deep crater of the land. The crater was in the middle of each of their regions untamed and untouched by any hint of civilization. It had been a month since they have explored the region together and they have yet yield any significant discovery except extending the topography. The group composed of a representative from other regions of the world, each of them a hand chosen talent. being picked among many to be part of this group. With that honor, came a sense of superiority. All of them had an attitude, and their personality constantly clashed on a daily basis. Their speciality consist of a scout, a foreman, a recorder, her the reporter. Viv the other girl from the group came from the southern region. While they were both female, the similarities end there. Where as Kari preferred planing for her action, Viv base her on actions on instincts without any basis or reasoning. What makes Viv hard to deal with is her spontaneity and selfishness. As she lead them down the path that was treacherous to all but most lithe human, namely only her. “A short cut” she casually remarked as she leap from one foothold to another as Kari “almost slip” far too many times to her liking. In the end Kari and the other two member demanded that Viv find them a safer route, but not before receiving the look fro Viv. The one you receive when someone sees you as incompetent. But what really draw the line, was when Viv decided to make Kari her mark. Kari had a necklace given to her by mother on her birthday. Viv and her cat-like eyes on it, the moment they met, just a couple days ago, the necklace vanish during her sleep. With only the four of them in the middle of nowhere, it narrows down the list of suspect to three with only one of them coming from a nation where kleptomania as a common trait among their people. Arnold the representative from the west was the foreman and in charge of supplies, and begun to order the other three members as if they were in the military corp. What he failed to understand was none of them came from military, and none of them respond well with bellowing and spit droplets landing on their face when he came to close to their face. Now that Kari thought about it perhaps all this barking and chest pounding was needed for him, as Arnold had never resorted to physicals force to her. But it was certainly not by choice, he was given specific instructions that he was not to harm any of the members to risk international conflict. They all were instructed the same. Whenever Kari questioned his methodology, she could see it took every strain of muscle on his body to hold back the urge to resort to violence, which she no doubt is second nature to him. Kari was not afraid, all she saw was a overbearingly man, whose only methodology was using force to get obedience. It seem that today his energy was focus on the final member Garret for avoiding. Garret, the representative from the north, who just shrug when Arnold growered him on, as if he were a mutt barking at him. Garret was the lazy one, who. There is in fact such a thing as being too passive. Kari could not figure out what the lanky man’s thoughts were. Garret was indeed a savant at mapping, but with social decorum were that of a hermit. All three of them had an ego, and Kari knows that she had one also. Arrogant, disobedient, and obsessive is what her colleague had described non too discreetly behind her back. She is aware of her flaw, and was trying to repress her nature for the sake of teamwork. That however could not be applied to the rest of the team mates. With each passing day with only each other as company. She was convinced that one of them was going to smoother them in their sleep and call it an accident. If this continues she may be the one that does to smothering.
